After years of loyal service to Third Echelon, Fisher discovers that the organization he trusted orchestrated the murder of his daughter Sarah. With nothing left to lose, he cuts all ties and wages a one-man war against the conspiracy that destroyed his life. No longer bound by rules of engagement, Fisher uses every brutal skill at his disposal — from savage interrogations to lethal close-quarters combat — to tear apart the shadow network from the inside.

KEY MISSIONS

Three operations that define Fisher's path from fugitive to the man who dismantled a shadow government.

01
AMERICAN EMBASSY — PRICE AIRFIELD
VALLETTA, MALTA
Fisher infiltrates the American Embassy in Malta chasing leads on his daughter's killer. What begins as reconnaissance erupts into a full-scale firefight. The projected text reads: 'FIND ANDRIY KOBIN' — smashed across embassy walls as Fisher tears through Black Arrow PMC operatives.
INFILTRATE EMBASSY LOCATE KOBIN EXTRACT INTEL
02
THE WHITE HOUSE — 1600 PENN
WASHINGTON, D.C.
An EMP detonation has crippled Washington. In total blackout, Third Echelon's rogue director Tom Reed attempts a coup against the President. Fisher breaches the White House through underground tunnels, fighting through Secret Service and Third Echelon operatives to reach the Oval Office and stop Reed's assassination plot.
BREACH WHITE HOUSE NEUTRALIZE REED SAVE PRESIDENT
03
THIRD ECHELON HQ
FORT MEADE, MARYLAND
Fisher assaults the very headquarters that trained him. Third Echelon's own fortress becomes his hunting ground. Every corridor he once walked is now a kill zone. The organization that made him will be unmade by him. Projected across the walls of the ops center: 'BURN IT DOWN.'
