Solid Snake's Mission

固蛇的任务

Two years after Shadow Moses, Solid Snake and Otacon, operating as the NGO "Philanthropy," intercept intelligence about a new Metal Gear being transported aboard a tanker through the Hudson River. Snake infiltrates the vessel USS Discovery during a storm, only to discover a Russian military faction led by Sergei Gurlukovich has the same target.

The mission culminates in the revelation of Metal Gear RAY — an anti-Metal Gear weapon designed by the Marines. Revolver Ocelot, under the influence of Liquid Snake's arm, seizes RAY and sinks the tanker. Snake is framed for the incident.

影子摩西事件两年后，固蛇和奥塔康以非政府组织"博爱"的名义，截获了一艘油轮通过哈德逊河运输新型Metal Gear的情报。暴风雨中，蛇潜入USS Discovery号，却发现由古鲁科维奇率领的俄罗斯军事派系也盯上了同一目标。

任务最终揭示了Metal Gear RAY——海军陆战队设计的反Metal Gear武器。受液蛇之臂影响的山猫夺取了RAY并击沉了油轮。蛇成为了替罪羊。

AFTERMATH // COVER-UP后续 // 掩盖真相

The Big Shell

大壳

Following the tanker sinking, a massive offshore decontamination facility — the "Big Shell" — was constructed over the wreck site, ostensibly to clean up the environmental disaster. The truth was far more sinister: the Big Shell was a cover for Arsenal Gear, a mobile fortress housing the Patriots' AI system GW.

The facility's hexagonal structure, connected by bridges over open water, would become the stage for the S3 Plan — an experiment in information control that would question the very nature of free will.

油轮沉没后，一个大型离岸净化设施——"大壳"——在沉船地点上方建造，表面上是为了清理环境灾难。真相远比表面更加阴暗：大壳是兵工厂装备的掩护，一座容纳爱国者AI系统GW的移动要塞。

这座由桥梁连接、架设于水面之上的六角形设施，将成为S3计划的舞台——一场质疑自由意志本质的信息控制实验。

NODE 01 // INFILTRATION渗透

Raiden's Insertion

雷电的潜入

FOXHOUND agent Raiden — real name Jack — is deployed to the Big Shell after the facility is seized by the terrorist group "Sons of Liberty," led by a man calling himself Solid Snake. Guided by his commanding officer, "Colonel Campbell," Raiden's mission mirrors Shadow Moses in disturbing ways. This is by design.

FOXHOUND特工雷电——真名杰克——在大壳被自称"自由之子"的恐怖组织占领后被派往该设施。在指挥官"坎贝尔上校"的指导下，雷电的任务以令人不安的方式映射着影子摩西事件。这一切都是设计好的。

NODE 02 // DECEPTION欺骗

Layers of Lies

谎言的层层

Nothing at the Big Shell is as it seems. The "Colonel" is an AI construct. The hostage situation is orchestrated. Raiden's girlfriend Rose is used as emotional leverage. The entire mission is a controlled simulation — the S3 Plan, designed to prove that any individual can be molded into a soldier through information control.

大壳中没有什么是表面看起来的样子。"上校"是AI构造体。人质事件是精心策划的。雷电的女友罗丝被用作情感杠杆。整个任务是一场受控模拟——S3计划，旨在证明任何个体都可以通过信息控制被塑造成士兵。

NODE 03 // REVELATION真相

The Real Snake

真正的蛇

The real Solid Snake has been operating undercover within the Big Shell as "Iroquois Pliskin." Together with Raiden, he works to uncover the truth behind the facility: Arsenal Gear lies beneath, carrying enough nuclear warheads to hold the world hostage, all controlled by the La-Li-Lu-Le-Lo.

真正的固蛇一直以"伊洛魁·普利斯金"的身份在大壳内卧底行动。他与雷电一起揭开设施背后的真相：兵工厂装备就在下方，携带着足以威胁全世界的核弹头，全部由La-Li-Lu-Le-Lo控制。

Arsenal Gear & The Patriots AI

Arsenal Gear

兵工厂装备

Arsenal Gear is a submersible mobile fortress hidden beneath the Big Shell. It carries a massive nuclear arsenal and an army of mass-produced Metal Gear RAY units. But its true weapon is GW — an AI designed to control the flow of digital information across the world, filtering truth from fiction, shaping public opinion, and ensuring the Patriots' invisible rule continues unchallenged.

兵工厂装备是隐藏在大壳之下的可潜水移动要塞。它携带着大量核武库和大规模生产的Metal Gear RAY部队。但它真正的武器是GW——一个旨在控制全球数字信息流动的AI，过滤真假，塑造舆论，确保爱国者的隐形统治不受挑战。

The S3 Plan

S3计划

He was meant to be proof of concept.

"固蛇模拟"——或"社会理性选择"——是大壳事件背后的真正目的。爱国者设计了整个场景来复制影子摩西，证明语境和信息控制可以制造任何结果。雷电从来就不应该成为英雄。他只是一个概念验证。

ANALYSIS // META分析 // 元叙事

Themes: Information, Memes, Identity

主题：信息、模因、身份

Information Control

信息控制

MGS2 predicted the post-truth era two decades early. The Patriots' goal is not censorship but curation — deciding which truths survive and which are buried. In a world drowning in data, whoever controls the filter controls reality. The digital age made this inevitable.

MGS2比后真相时代早了二十年。爱国者的目标不是审查而是策展——决定哪些真相存续，哪些被掩埋。在数据泛滥的世界中，控制过滤器的人控制现实。数字时代使这一切不可避免。

Memes & Legacy

模因与遗产

Not internet memes — Richard Dawkins' original concept: cultural units of information that pass between people, shaping society. Genes carry biological legacy; memes carry cultural legacy. The Patriots seek to control which memes — which ideas, values, truths — survive into the future. What you leave behind is not what is engraved in stone, but what is woven into the lives of others.

不是互联网梗——而是理查德·道金斯的原始概念：在人与人之间传递、塑造社会的文化信息单元。基因承载生物遗产；模因承载文化遗产。爱国者试图控制哪些模因——哪些思想、价值观、真相——能够延续到未来。你留下的不是刻在石头上的东西，而是编织进他人生活中的东西。

Identity & Free Will

身份与自由意志

Are you who you choose to be, or who you are told to be? Raiden is literally constructed as a character — his mission, his codec support, even his girlfriend are part of the simulation. The game asks: if your choices are predetermined by the information you receive, are they truly your choices? And then it turns to you, the player, and asks the same question.

你是你选择成为的人，还是被告知成为的人？雷电作为角色被字面意义上地构建——他的任务、通讯支持，甚至他的女友都是模拟的一部分。游戏问道：如果你的选择是由你接收的信息预设的，那它们真的是你的选择吗？然后它转向你，玩家，问同样的问题。

We're all pawns, controlled by something greater: Memes. The DNA of the soul. They shape our will. They are the culture — they are everything we pass on. 我们都是棋子，被更伟大的东西控制着：模因。灵魂的DNA。它们塑造我们的意志。它们就是文化——就是我们传承的一切。

— SOLID SNAKE
