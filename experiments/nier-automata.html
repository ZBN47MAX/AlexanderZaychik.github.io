The World — 11945 AD

A planet without its creators
[BlackBox Rec No. 0927]

In the distant future, Earth has been invaded by alien machines. Humanity fled to the Moon, leaving behind android soldiers — the YoRHa units — to fight an endless proxy war against the machine lifeforms that now dominate the ruined surface.

But the truth is far more painful than the lie. The Council of Humanity on the Moon is a fiction. Humanity is extinct. The androids fight for a cause that no longer exists — and the machines they destroy may be more alive than anyone admits.
