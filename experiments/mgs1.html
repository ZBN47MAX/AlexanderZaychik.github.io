MISSION BRIEFING

In February 2005, a renegade special forces unit, FOXHOUND, seized a nuclear weapons disposal facility on Shadow Moses Island in Alaska's Fox Archipelago.

They are threatening the White House with a nuclear strike using a weapon known as Metal Gear REX unless they receive the remains of Big Boss and a ransom of $1 billion within 24 hours.

Solid Snake, legendary soldier and former FOXHOUND operative, has been pulled out of retirement for this mission. His objectives: rescue the hostages, neutralize the nuclear threat, and eliminate Metal Gear REX.
