02

THE LOOP

BLACKREEF ISLAND

THE ISLAND

Blackreef is a mysterious island trapped in a time loop. Once a military installation, it has become the playground of the AEON Program — a hedonistic society of Visionaries who exploit the loop to live without consequence. Every day resets. Every death is undone. Every sin is forgotten.

COLT & JULIANNA

Colt Vahn wakes on Blackreef's shores with no memory and one goal: break the loop by eliminating all eight Visionaries in a single day. Standing in his way is Julianna Blake — the loop's self-appointed guardian. She'll hunt Colt relentlessly, invading his timeline to ensure the loop endures. Their rivalry is personal, adversarial, and far more complex than either suspects.

SLAB POWERS

SUPERNATURAL ABILITIES

⇄

SHIFT

Teleport short distances instantly. Cross gaps, reach vantage points, and close the distance on enemies in the blink of an eye.

☍

NEXUS

Link enemies together so they share the same fate. Eliminate one, and all connected targets fall with them.

◌

AETHER

Become nearly invisible to enemies. Slip past guards, set up the perfect ambush, or escape when things go wrong.

⚛

KARNESIS

Seize enemies with telekinetic force. Hurl them off cliffs, into each other, or suspend them helplessly in mid-air.

★

HAVOC

Channel raw energy into devastating power. Absorb massive damage and unleash it back, becoming an unstoppable force.

DISTRICTS

EXPLORE BLACKREEF

UPDAAM

The cultural heart of Blackreef. Grand architecture, bustling streets, and Aleksis Dorsey's imposing mansion overlooking it all.

KARL'S BAY

Industrial docks and derelict hangars. Harriet's cult gathers here in the morning. A place of faith and firepower.

FRISTAD ROCK

Windswept cliffs and Frank's hidden bunker-studio. Fia's reactor hums beneath the surface. Beauty masking danger.

THE COMPLEX

The scientific nerve center where the loop was born. Dr. Wenjie's domain. Cold corridors and dangerous experiments.
