MILITAIRES SANS FRONTIÈRES 无国界军队

⚔

Soldiers Without Borders无国界的士兵

MSF is a private military company founded by Big Boss and Kazuhira Miller in 1974. Operating from a offshore platform in the Caribbean, they take on missions regardless of national allegiance or ideology. MSF是Big Boss和和泉一平（Kazuhira Miller）于1974年创立的私人军事公司。从加勒比海的海上平台出发，他们不分国家忠诚或意识形态地执行任务。

🌎

Costa Rica Operation哥斯达黎加行动

A mysterious armed force has invaded Costa Rica, a nation without a military. Big Boss is contracted to investigate, uncovering a Cold War conspiracy involving nuclear deterrence and artificial intelligence. 一支神秘的武装力量入侵了没有军队的哥斯达黎加。Big Boss受雇进行调查，揭露了一个涉及核威慑和人工智能的冷战阴谋。

✈

Nuclear Deterrence核威慑

At the heart of the crisis lies the question: can a machine decide to launch a nuclear strike? The Peace Walker AI is designed to provide a perfect, emotionless nuclear deterrent -- removing the human factor entirely. 危机的核心问题是：机器能否决定发动核打击？和平行者AI旨在提供完美、无情感的核威慑——完全消除人为因素。

MISSION BRIEFINGS 任务简报

MISSION 01任务 01

An Army Without Borders无国界的军队

Ramón Gálvez Mena and Paz Ortega arrive at MSF's base. They bring evidence of a mysterious armed force occupying parts of Costa Rica and request Big Boss's help to drive them out. 拉蒙·加尔韦斯·梅纳和帕斯·奥尔特加抵达MSF基地。他们带来了神秘武装力量占领哥斯达黎加部分地区的证据，请求Big Boss的帮助。

MISSION 02任务 02

Jungle Infiltration丛林渗透

Big Boss enters the Costa Rican jungle alone. The lush tropical terrain hides enemy patrols, supply depots, and the first clues about the AI weapons project. Big Boss独自进入哥斯达黎加丛林。茂密的热带地形隐藏着敌方巡逻队、补给站以及AI武器项目的第一批线索。

MISSION 03任务 03

The Pupa Encounter蛹之遭遇

The first AI weapon reveals itself -- the Pupa, a beetle-like unmanned weapon. Big Boss must defeat this prototype to learn about the larger Peace Walker project. 第一台AI兵器现身——蛹，一种甲虫状的无人兵器。Big Boss必须击败这台原型机以了解更大规模的和平行者计划。

MISSION 04任务 04

The Chrysalis Takes Flight蝶蛹升空

A flying AI weapon, the Chrysalis, dominates the skies above Costa Rica. This airborne threat requires coordinated co-op tactics and heavy firepower to bring down. The battle marks a turning point in understanding the enemy's true capabilities. 飞行AI兵器蝶蛹统治着哥斯达黎加的天空。这个空中威胁需要协调的合作战术和强大火力才能击落。这场战斗标志着理解敌人真正实力的转折点。

💾 TAPE: AI ANALYSIS磁带：AI分析
MISSION 05任务 05

Coldman's Gambit科尔德曼的赌局

Hot Coldman, CIA Station Chief, reveals his plan to launch a live nuclear strike to prove deterrence theory. Big Boss must race to stop the countdown. CIA站长霍特·科尔德曼揭示了他发射实弹核打击以证明威慑理论的计划。Big Boss必须争分夺秒阻止倒计时。

MISSION 06任务 06

Peace Walker Rising和平行者觉醒

The final battle. Peace Walker, carrying The Boss's AI, marches toward the missile base. In its final moments, The Boss's will overrides the machine, choosing peace -- walking into the sea. 最终之战。搭载The Boss之AI的和平行者向导弹基地进发。在最后时刻，The Boss的意志覆盖了机器，选择和平——走入大海。

Peace Walker was designed from the ground up for cooperative play. Up to four players can tackle boss battles against the massive AI weapons, share supplies, perform cooperative actions like synchronized takedowns, and coordinate strategies using the in-game communication system. The AI weapon battles are specifically designed to require teamwork -- flanking, drawing fire, and timing attacks together. 和平行者从底层开始就为合作游戏而设计。最多四名玩家可以合力对抗巨型AI兵器Boss战，共享物资，执行同步击倒等协作动作，并使用游戏内通信系统协调策略。AI兵器战斗专门设计为需要团队合作——包抄、吸引火力以及协调攻击时机。
