△

革命性硬件 REVOLUTIONARY HARDWARE

搭载 33MHz R3000A 处理器与专用 GPU，PS1 是第一台真正将 3D 图形带入千家万户的游戏主机。它证明了光盘媒介可以取代卡带，彻底改变了游戏的分发方式。 Powered by a 33MHz R3000A CPU and a dedicated GPU, the PS1 was the first console to truly bring 3D graphics to living rooms worldwide. It proved that CD media could replace cartridges, revolutionizing game distribution.

○

游戏库 GAME LIBRARY

超过 3000 款游戏发行，涵盖了从 RPG 到动作冒险的所有类型。《最终幻想VII》、《合金装备》、《生化危机》等传奇IP均诞生于这个平台。 Over 3,000 titles spanning every genre from RPG to action-adventure. Legendary franchises like Final Fantasy VII, Metal Gear Solid, and Resident Evil were born on this platform.

□

文化影响 CULTURAL IMPACT

PS1 销量超过 1.02 亿台，是史上首款突破一亿销量的游戏主机。它将电子游戏从"儿童玩具"提升为主流娱乐形式，深刻影响了流行文化。 With over 102 million units sold, the PS1 was the first console to break the 100-million barrier. It elevated gaming from a children's hobby to mainstream entertainment, profoundly shaping popular culture.

发展历程 TIMELINE
从诞生到传奇 FROM BIRTH TO LEGEND
1994
12 月 3 日，PlayStation 在日本首发，售价 39,800 日元。首发日即售出超过 10 万台，引发抢购热潮。索尼正式进入游戏行业。 December 3rd — PlayStation launches in Japan at ¥39,800. Over 100,000 units sold on day one. Sony officially enters the gaming industry.
1995
9 月在北美发售，定价 $299，比世嘉土星低 $100。E3 展会上久多良木健仅用一句"$299"就赢得了全场。 September launch in North America at $299 — $100 less than Sega Saturn. At E3, Ken Kutaragi won the crowd with a single word: "$299."
1997
《最终幻想VII》发售，被誉为游戏史上最重要的作品之一。它不仅推动了 PS1 的销量暴涨，更将 RPG 推广到了全球玩家群体。同年，DualShock 手柄发布，引入双摇杆和振动反馈。 Final Fantasy VII launches, widely regarded as one of the most important games ever made. It drove PS1 sales to new heights and brought RPGs to a global audience. The DualShock controller also debuts, introducing dual analog sticks and vibration feedback.
2000
PS2 发售，PS1 逐步退居幕后。但 PS1 游戏仍可在 PS2 上向下兼容运行，延续了它的生命力。 PS2 launches and the PS1 begins its graceful exit. However, PS1 games remain playable on PS2 via backward compatibility, extending its legacy.
2006
PS1 正式停产，累计销量超过 1.02 亿台。它不仅是一台游戏机，更是一整代人的青春记忆。 PS1 production officially ends at over 102 million units sold. More than a console — it was the defining memory of an entire generation.
