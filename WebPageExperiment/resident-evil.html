☣ THE UMBRELLA CORPORATION 保护伞公司 ☣

Founded in 1968 by Oswell E. Spencer, Edward Ashford, and James Marcus, the Umbrella Corporation presented itself as a leading pharmaceutical enterprise. Behind the facade of vaccines and consumer healthcare products, Umbrella conducted illegal biological weapons research in hidden underground laboratories spanning multiple continents.

保护伞公司由奥斯维尔·E·斯宾塞、爱德华·阿什福德和詹姆斯·马库斯于1968年创立，表面上是一家领先的制药企业。在疫苗和消费者保健产品的幌子后面，保护伞在遍布多个大陆的隐秘地下实验室中进行非法生物武器研究。

The discovery of the Progenitor Virus in West Africa's Ndipaya tribe set in motion a chain of events that would threaten all human life. Project W. Subject selection. The T-Virus. The Raccoon City Incident. Every step was calculated, every outbreak a field test for their true objective: the complete restructuring of the human species.

在西非恩迪帕亚部落发现的始祖病毒引发了一系列威胁全人类的事件。W计划。实验体筛选。T病毒。浣熊市事件。每一步都经过精心计算，每一次疫情爆发都是他们真正目标的实地测试：人类物种的彻底重构。

☣ VIRUS FILES 病毒档案 ☣

T-VIRUS
TYRANT VIRUS — CLASS A 暴君病毒 — A级

Derived from the Progenitor Virus. Causes cellular necrosis and reanimation. Responsible for the Raccoon City outbreak. Subjects lose higher brain function while gaining enhanced durability and aggression. 源自始祖病毒。导致细胞坏死与复活。是浣熊市疫情的元凶。感染者丧失高级脑功能，但获得增强的耐久性和攻击性。

INFECTION RATE感染率85%
G-VIRUS
GOLGOTHA VIRUS — CLASS S 哥尔哥达病毒 — S级

Developed by William Birkin. Unlike the T-Virus, G causes uncontrollable mutagenic evolution. The host undergoes continuous, agonizing transformation, developing new limbs and sensory organs. Each mutation stage increases lethality exponentially. 由威廉·伯肯开发。与T病毒不同，G病毒导致不可控的突变进化。宿主经历持续的痛苦变形，发展出新的肢体和感觉器官。每个变异阶段的致命性呈指数级增长。

MUTATION RATE变异率95%
LAS PLAGAS
PARASITIC ORGANISM — CLASS A 寄生体 — A级

Ancient parasitic organism discovered in rural Spain. Unlike viruses, Plagas maintain host consciousness while overriding free will. Hosts (Ganados) can be controlled remotely. The parasite eventually replaces the host's nervous system entirely. 在西班牙农村发现的古代寄生生物。与病毒不同，普拉加在保留宿主意识的同时覆盖其自由意志。宿主（加纳多）可被远程控制。寄生虫最终会完全替代宿主的神经系统。

CONTROL RATE控制率72%
MOLD (MUTAMYCETE)
MYCOLOGICAL BIOWEAPON — CLASS S 真菌类生物武器 — S级

A megamycete-derived fungal superorganism. The Mold connects infected hosts into a hive consciousness controlled by a central Megamycete. Grants regeneration and biological manipulation abilities. Mother Miranda sought to use it to resurrect the dead. 一种源自超级菌体的真菌超级生物。菌体将感染宿主连接到由中央超级菌体控制的蜂巢意识中。赋予再生和生物操控能力。米兰达母亲试图用它来复活死者。

SPREAD RATE扩散率88%

☣ INCIDENT LOCATIONS 事件地点 ☣

SPENCER MANSION 斯宾塞公馆
ARKLAY MOUNTAINS — 1998

A Victorian estate hiding an underground laboratory. Designed by George Trevor, whose own family became test subjects. Every locked door, every puzzle, every shadow conceals something waiting to kill you. The mansion where survival horror was born. 一座隐藏着地下实验室的维多利亚式庄园。由乔治·特雷弗设计，而他自己的家人却成了实验对象。每扇锁着的门、每个谜题、每个阴影都隐藏着等待杀死你的东西。生存恐怖诞生的公馆。

R.P.D. STATION R.P.D. 警察局
RACCOON CITY — 1998

Once a grand art museum converted into a police station. During the outbreak, it became a tomb. The echoing halls filled with the shuffling dead. Mr. X's footsteps overhead. The Goddess statue watches over the fallen. Chief Irons' dark secret festers in the basement. 曾经是一座宏伟的艺术博物馆，被改建为警察局。在疫情爆发期间，它变成了一座坟墓。回荡的大厅里满是蹒跚的死者。头顶上是暴君先生的脚步声。女神雕像注视着倒下的人。艾恩斯局长的黑暗秘密在地下室中溃烂。

THE VILLAGE 村庄
EASTERN EUROPE — 2021

A snow-covered nightmare nestled in the mountains. Four lords rule under Mother Miranda: Dimitrescu's gothic castle, Beneviento's haunted dollhouse, Moreau's flooded reservoir, Heisenberg's industrial factory. The village itself is overrun with Lycans howling in the perpetual twilight. 一个坐落在群山中被雪覆盖的噩梦。四位领主在米兰达母亲的统治下：蒂米特雷斯库的哥特式城堡、贝尼维恩托的闹鬼玩偶屋、莫罗的淹没水库、海森堡的工业工厂。村庄本身被在永恒暮色中嚎叫的狼人所占据。

BAKER RESIDENCE 贝克庄园
DULVEY, LOUISIANA — 2017

A rotting plantation house in the Louisiana bayou. The Baker family — Jack, Marguerite, Lucas — were once ordinary people before Eveline's Mold infection twisted them into sadistic monsters. The dinner table is set. The food is unspeakable. Welcome to the family, son. 路易斯安那州沼泽中一座腐朽的种植园房屋。贝克一家——杰克、玛格丽特、卢卡斯——在伊芙琳的菌体感染将他们扭曲成虐待狂怪物之前，曾经是普通人。餐桌已经摆好。食物不堪入目。欢迎加入这个家庭，儿子。
