Chapter I

The Hotel

⚷

Perched on the eastern hills of Kyoto, overlooking the city's ancient temple rooftops, the Moonview Grand Hotel has stood in perfect symmetry since 1965. Founded by the reclusive aesthete Mr. Tanaka Hideo, the hotel was designed so that every corridor, every doorframe, every folded towel achieves a state of absolute visual harmony.

The hotel's motto — ‘Order is beauty, and beauty is order’ — is inscribed in gold leaf above the lobby desk, where the Lobby Boy stands at attention from 6:00 AM to midnight, hat perfectly level, buttons perfectly polished.

EST. 1965 — KYOTO

  • I All personal belongings must be arranged symmetrically within one's room at all times.
  • II Breakfast is served at precisely 7:30 AM. Tardiness is noted but forgiven — once.
  • III The lobby piano may only be played in the key of C major before noon.
