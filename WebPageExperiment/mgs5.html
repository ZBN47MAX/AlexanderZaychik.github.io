In 1984, the legendary soldier Venom Snake awakens from a nine-year coma to find his world shattered. Driven by an insatiable thirst for revenge, he rebuilds his private military force — Diamond Dogs — from the ashes of Militaires Sans Frontieres. Operating from the offshore fortress Mother Base, Snake and his commander Kazuhira Miller wage a shadow war across the war-torn landscapes of Afghanistan and the Angola-Zaire border.

◇ THE ENEMY ◇
The Man Who Stole Our Past
SKULL FACE
COMMANDER — XOF SPECIAL FORCES
A man consumed by vengeance against language itself. Horrifically burned and rebuilt, Skull Face commands the rogue unit XOF and wields the vocal cord parasites — biological weapons that can exterminate entire languages. His ultimate weapon: the bipedal nuclear-armed Metal Gear Sahelanthropus, piloted by the psychic child Tretij Rebenok.
THREAT ASSESSMENT
XOF Special Forces
The mirror image of FOX — Cipher's black-ops unit responsible for the destruction of MSF and the nine-year coma of Big Boss. Operating outside any nation's authority.
BIOLOGICAL THREAT
Vocal Cord Parasites
Parasites that target speakers of specific languages. When activated, the host's vocal cords are consumed. Skull Face's weapon to create a world of silence — where only nuclear deterrence speaks.
