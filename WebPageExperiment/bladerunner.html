// Wallace Corporation

Technology of Gods

Niander Wallace saved civilization from famine, then bought Tyrell's legacy. Now he builds life itself — and dreams of making it multiply beyond his control.

01
Nexus-9 Program

The latest generation of replicants. Perfectly obedient, incapable of defiance — or so the programming claims. Built with open-ended lifespans and implanted memories designed by artisans.

02
Synthetic Agriculture

Wallace's genetically modified crops ended the global famine after the Blackout. This single achievement gave him the power and resources to acquire Tyrell Corporation's remaining assets.

03
Memory Fabrication

Dr. Ana Stelline designs memories for replicants in a sterile bubble — dreams of birthday parties, childhood rain, a mother's touch. Fictions that feel more real than most humans' lives.

04
Off-World Colonization

Nine colonies thrive beyond Earth, built on replicant labor. Wallace needs replicants that can reproduce — an army that builds itself. The final frontier of his ambition.

Key Locations

From rain-soaked megalopolis to irradiated desert, each landscape tells the story of a world abandoned by hope.

Los Angeles
34.0522° N, 118.2437° W

Perpetual rain. Neon-lit towers pierce a poisoned sky. Millions crowd the lower levels while the wealthy ascend. K's beat — the city that never sees the sun.

Las Vegas
36.1699° N, 115.1398° W

A radioactive amber wasteland. Colossal statues stand half-buried in sand. In the ruins of a casino, a ghost from the past waits with a dog and a glass of whiskey.

San Diego
32.7157° N, 117.1611° W

The Trash Mesa. A massive scrapyard where orphans sift through the refuse of civilization. Here, among the discarded, a resistance quietly grows.
