亡霊の道

The Ghost's Path

The Invasion

In 1274, the Mongol Empire launched a devastating assault on Tsushima Island. Eighty samurai rode to Komoda Beach to face an army of thousands. They fell. The island burned. Honor demanded a frontal charge; reality demanded something else entirely.

Jin Sakai

The last surviving samurai of Clan Sakai. Raised by Lord Shimura to uphold the samurai code — to fight with honor, to face enemies head-on, to die before dishonor. But the Mongols do not fight with honor. And the dead cannot protect anyone.

Becoming the Ghost

To save his people, Jin must abandon everything he was taught. Poison. Stealth. Fear as a weapon. The Ghost is born — not from darkness, but from the desperate love of a man who would sacrifice his soul for his home.

戦闘の型

Combat Stances

石

Stone

Strong against swordsmen. Heavy overhead strikes break through defenses like water splitting stone.

水

Water

Effective against shieldmen. Fluid, surging strikes flow around barriers like a river finding its path.

風

Wind

Devastates spearmen. Swift kicks and typhoon strikes scatter polearm users like leaves in a gale.

月

Moon

Overcomes brutes. Spinning, powerful strikes stagger even the largest foes beneath the cold moonlight.

Mythic Tales

壱

The Legend of Tadayori

An unmatched archer whose legendary skill with the half bow became the stuff of whispered tales. His armor awaits one worthy enough to claim it.

弐

The Curse of Uchitsune

A bow of terrible power, sealed away for the madness it inflicts upon its wielder. Some weapons demand more than skill — they demand your sanity.

参

The Six Blades of Kojiro

Five legendary straw-hat ronin guard the path to Kojiro, the greatest swordsman who ever lived. Defeat them all, and face the master himself.
