Mission Intel

Operation Briefing

VIRTUOUS MISSION

The First HALO Jump

August 24, 1964. Naked Snake performs the world's first HALO (High Altitude Low Opening) jump into the Soviet jungle of Tselinoyarsk. His mission: rescue the defecting Soviet scientist Nikolai Sokolov, developer of the Shagohod nuclear-armed tank. This is where the legend begins.

SNAKE EATER

Operation Snake Eater

One week later. After The Boss's defection to the Soviet Union, Snake must return to Tselinoyarsk to eliminate the Cobra Unit, destroy the Shagohod, kill Colonel Volgin, and ultimately face his former mentor — The Boss herself. America's honor and the fate of the Cold War hang in the balance.

The Cold War Context

At the height of nuclear tension between superpowers, a rogue Soviet colonel named Volgin has seized a portable nuclear device — the Davy Crockett. When The Boss defects and delivers two miniature nuclear warheads to Volgin, the world teeters on the brink of nuclear war. Only one man can prevent Armageddon.

Support Team

Major Zero (mission command), Para-Medic (medical and save data), Sigint (weapons and technology), and the ever-mysterious EVA — a double agent whose true allegiance remains one of the game's deepest mysteries. Together they guide Snake through the most perilous operation of the Cold War.

Enemy Combatants

The Cobra Unit

A legendary special forces unit from World War II, each member carries an emotion into battle that defines them. They are the greatest warriors of the 20th century.

🐝
The Pain
EMOTION: PAIN

Master of hornets and bees. The Pain controls swarms of bullet bees through pheromones, using them as living armor, projectiles, and even a Tommy gun-shaped formation. His body is covered in stingers, and he feels nothing but agony — his emotion in battle.

🕷
The Fear
EMOTION: FEAR

A spider-like assassin with supernatural agility. The Fear can leap between trees, turn nearly invisible with stealth camouflage, and fires poison-tipped crossbow bolts. His dislocated joints allow inhuman contortions. He instills primal terror in all who face him.

🌳
The End
EMOTION: THE END

The legendary 'Father of Sniping' — over 100 years old. The End photosynthesizes like a plant, able to remain motionless for weeks. His sniper duel across three massive jungle zones is one of gaming's greatest boss battles. He and his parrot share an ancient, symbiotic bond with the forest.

🔥
The Fury
EMOTION: FURY

A former cosmonaut driven insane by the void of space. The Fury wields a flamethrower and jet pack, turning underground tunnels into an inferno. Having stared into the infinite darkness above Earth, he returned consumed by unquenchable rage and the desire to burn everything.

💧
The Sorrow
EMOTION: SORROW

A spirit medium who communes with the dead — killed by The Boss years before the events of Snake Eater. The Sorrow appears as a ghost, forcing Snake to walk a river of every soldier he has killed. His 'battle' is a moral reckoning, not a fight. He was The Boss's lover and Ocelot's father.

★
The Boss
THE JOY — LEADER OF THE COBRA UNIT

The greatest warrior of the 20th century. The Boss — codenamed 'The Joy' — is the mother of the Special Forces, Snake's mentor, and the true hero of the story. She stormed the beaches of Normandy while pregnant, developed CQC with Snake, and sacrificed everything for her country. Her 'defection' was a mission — she was ordered to die at Snake's hands to prevent nuclear war. Her white horse, her field of white flowers, her scar — she is the soul of Metal Gear Solid 3.

The Camouflage system lets Snake blend with the environment. Different camo patterns and face paints affect the Camo Index — higher values mean better concealment. Mastering this system is essential for stealth in the jungle.

The Cure system requires Snake to treat injuries in the field. Burns, broken bones, bullet wounds, and leech bites all require specific treatments. Untreated injuries reduce maximum Life and impair movement.

  • Bullet Wound → Knife (dig out bullet) + Disinfectant + Styptic + Bandage + Suture
  • Broken Bone → Splint + Bandage
  • Burn → Ointment + Bandage
  • Cut/Slash → Disinfectant + Styptic + Bandage + Suture
  • Leech → Cigar burn (to remove) + Disinfectant + Styptic
  • Arrow/Bolt → Knife (extract) + Disinfectant + Styptic + Bandage
  • Poison → Antidote or Serum

Snake must hunt, forage, and capture wildlife to maintain his Stamina gauge. Food rots over time. Eating rotten food or poisonous wildlife causes stomach aches. The jungle provides — if you know what to eat.

Weapons & CQC

PISTOL

M1911A1

The iconic .45 caliber pistol. Custom-engraved, it becomes Snake's signature sidearm. Suppressor-compatible. The Boss carries a matching model — the weapon that defines their bond.

SNIPER

SVD Dragunov

Soviet semi-automatic sniper rifle. Essential for the legendary duel with The End. 7.62x54mmR caliber with PSO-1 scope. The weapon of patience and precision in the jungle.

ASSAULT

Avtomat Kalashnikova. The most reliable assault rifle ever made. Found throughout Tselinoyarsk as the standard Soviet weapon. Rugged, powerful, and fitting for the jungle environment.

The tranquilizer pistol for non-lethal operations. Suppressed and silent, it embodies the stealth approach. Essential for capturing food animals alive and achieving a no-kill run.

The Truth

The Boss's Legacy

The Boss was never a traitor. Her defection was ordered by the U.S. government — a sacrificial mission to retrieve the Philosophers' Legacy, a vast fortune accumulated by the most powerful minds of the 20th century. She was ordered to die at the hands of her own apprentice to provide America plausible deniability for Volgin's nuclear strike.

In a field of white flowers, Snake fulfilled his mission. He pulled the trigger on the woman who taught him everything — his mother in spirit, his mentor, his greatest enemy and greatest ally. The Boss died a hero, branded a traitor. Only Snake knew the truth.

For this, he was awarded the title of Big Boss — the greatest soldier in the world. But the title meant nothing without her. At her grave in Arlington National Cemetery, he shed his final tears and began his transformation from hero into the legend that would shape the world for decades to come.

She was a true patriot.
