圣巢的传说 The Lore of Hallownest

一个被虚空与光辉所撕裂的王国 A Kingdom Torn Between Void and Radiance

圣巢，曾是一个繁荣的地下王国，由苍白之王统治。这位神秘的高等存在赋予了虫族心智与文明，建造了宏伟的城市与精巧的道路系统。然而，辉煌之下潜藏着致命的威胁——辐光，一位被遗忘的远古之神，在梦境中散播瘟疫般的感染之光。 Hallownest was once a prosperous underground kingdom, ruled by the Pale King. This mysterious higher being granted the bugs of the land mind and civilization, constructing grand cities and intricate road systems. But beneath the glory lurked a deadly threat — the Radiance, a forgotten ancient god, spreading a plague-like infection through dreams.

为了对抗这场灾难，苍白之王做出了残酷的决定：在深渊之中创造纯粹容器——空洞骑士——来封印辐光。然而，被选中的容器并非真正的"空洞"。一丝本不该存在的意念，一缕情感的裂痕，最终导致封印逐渐崩溃，王国再次陷入沉沦。 To combat this catastrophe, the Pale King made a cruel decision: within the Abyss, he created a Pure Vessel — the Hollow Knight — to seal the Radiance. But the chosen vessel was not truly "hollow." A flicker of thought that should not have existed, a fracture of emotion, eventually caused the seal to slowly crumble, and the kingdom fell once more.

如今，你以一个无名小虫的身份来到这片废墟。探索被感染侵蚀的隧道，面对堕落的守护者，收集远古的护符与力量。你的旅途不仅是一场冒险，更是关于牺牲、遗忘与存在意义的沉思。 Now, you arrive at these ruins as a nameless little bug. Explore tunnels corroded by infection, face fallen guardians, collect ancient charms and powers. Your journey is not merely an adventure — it is a meditation on sacrifice, oblivion, and the meaning of existence.

圣巢的疆域 Realms of Hallownest

六个截然不同的地下世界 Six Distinct Underground Worlds

⚔

遗忘十字路Forgotten Crossroads

FORGOTTEN CROSSROADS 遗忘十字路

圣巢的入口与交通枢纽。曾经繁忙的通道如今被感染的虫族占据，到处是橙色的脓包与失去心智的游荡者。这里是每个旅者踏入深渊的第一步。 The entrance and crossroads hub of Hallownest. Once-busy passages are now occupied by infected bugs, littered with orange pustules and mindless wanderers. This is every traveler's first step into the abyss.

🌿

苍绿之径Greenpath

GREENPATH 苍绿之径

被茂密植被覆盖的地下丛林，酸液池遍布其间。这里生命力旺盛却危机四伏，是大黄蜂守护的领域。苔藓与藤蔓间回荡着远古的低语。 An underground jungle blanketed in lush vegetation, with pools of acid scattered throughout. Vibrant with life yet fraught with peril, this is Hornet's guarded domain. Ancient whispers echo through moss and vine.

🌧

泪水之城City of Tears

CITY OF TEARS 泪水之城

圣巢的首都，永恒的雨幕从洞穴顶端倾泻而下。宏伟的蓝色建筑与空旷的广场诉说着昔日的荣光。灵魂圣所与守望者之塔矗立其中，守护着被遗忘的秘密。 The capital of Hallownest, where an eternal curtain of rain cascades from the cavern ceiling. Grand blue architecture and empty plazas speak of bygone glory. The Soul Sanctum and Watcher's Spire stand guard over forgotten secrets.

🕸

深邃巢穴Deepnest

DEEPNEST 深邃巢穴

圣巢最黑暗、最危险的区域。蜘蛛与噩梦般的生物栖息于无尽的黑暗之中。这里几乎没有光源，每一步都可能是陷阱。大黄蜂的母亲赫拉曾是这里的统治者。 The darkest and most dangerous region of Hallownest. Spiders and nightmarish creatures dwell in endless darkness. Almost devoid of light, every step could be a trap. Hornet's mother, Herrah, once ruled here.

💎

水晶山峰Crystal Peak

CRYSTAL PEAK 水晶山峰

闪烁着粉红色光芒的矿脉遍布此地。被感染的矿工仍在机械般地挖掘着，水晶的微光照亮了这片不祥的矿区。水晶守护者在最高处沉默地等待着入侵者。 Pink-glowing crystal veins run throughout this region. Infected miners still dig mechanically, the crystal's glow illuminating this ominous mining district. The Crystal Guardian waits silently at the summit for intruders.

⬛

深渊The Abyss

THE ABYSS 深渊

圣巢的最底层，虚空之物的诞生地。无数失败的容器在此沉眠，它们的残影化为虚空的暗流。这里是苍白之王最深的秘密，也是小骑士真正的起源之地。 The deepest layer of Hallownest, birthplace of void beings. Countless failed vessels slumber here, their remnants flowing as void currents. This holds the Pale King's deepest secret — and the Knight's true origin.
