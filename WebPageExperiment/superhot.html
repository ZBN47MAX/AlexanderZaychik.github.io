ABOUT THE SYSTEM

SUPERHOT is the FPS where time moves only when you move. No regenerating health bars. No ammo drops. It's just you, outnumbered and outgunned, grabbing weapons off fallen enemies to shoot, slice, and maneuver through a blizzard of slow-motion bullets.

Blurring the lines between cautious strategy and unbridled mayhem, SUPERHOT is the definitive FPS experience that demands your creativity and wit. The game challenges you to rethink everything you know about first-person shooters.

Developed by the SUPERHOT Team, the game transcends the boundaries of traditional gaming with its meta-narrative about control, obedience, and the nature of gaming itself.

// SYSTEM.MECHANICS

CORE MECHANICS

01

TIME MANIPULATION

Time advances only when you move. Stand still and the world freezes — a ballet of bullets suspended in air, waiting for your next decision.

02

WEAPON THROWING

Out of ammo? Throw your weapon at the enemy. Every object becomes a projectile. Katanas, bottles, ashtrays — anything can turn the tide of battle.

03

HOTSWITCH

Transfer your consciousness into an enemy's body. Take control of their form, their weapon, their position. The ultimate tactical advantage — become your enemy.

04

MIND CONTROL

The system wants you to play. It needs you to play. The narrative blurs reality and simulation, questioning who truly controls whom in this digital prison.

05

ONE-HIT FRAGILITY

One hit and you shatter. The crystalline red enemies shatter too. This fragility creates extreme tension — every encounter is life or death, every move calculated.

06

ENDLESS MODE

After the story, endless waves of enemies test your mastery. Challenge mode strips away abilities. Speedrun mode demands perfection. The system never lets you go.

// SYSTEM.CHRONOLOGY

TIMELINE

2013

7DFPS GAME JAM

SUPERHOT was born as a prototype in the 7-Day FPS challenge. The core mechanic — time moves when you move — captivated the gaming world instantly.

2014

KICKSTARTER SUCCESS

The crowdfunding campaign reached its goal in under 24 hours, ultimately raising over $250,000. The community had spoken — they wanted more.

2016

FULL RELEASE

SUPERHOT launched on PC to critical acclaim. 'The most innovative shooter I've played in years' became the defining quote of a generation of FPS games.

2017

SUPERHOT VR

The VR adaptation redefined virtual reality gaming. Physically dodging bullets and throwing objects brought the time-manipulation mechanic to life like never before.

2020

MIND CONTROL DELETE

The standalone expansion expanded the SUPERHOT universe with roguelike elements, new abilities, and deeper exploration of the system's hold on the player.
