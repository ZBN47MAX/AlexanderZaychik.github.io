Black Mesa Research Facility

Black Mesa

THE RESONANCE CASCADE

Black Mesa Research Facility — a top-secret government laboratory hidden deep within the New Mexico desert. On May 16, 200-, a routine experiment in the Anomalous Materials department went catastrophically wrong. The resulting Resonance Cascade tore open a dimensional rift, unleashing alien creatures from the border world Xen.

Dr. Gordon Freeman, a theoretical physicist armed with only a crowbar and the prototype HEV Mark IV suit, fought through headcrabs, Vortigaunts, and military cover-up forces to reach the Lambda Complex — and ultimately, Xen itself.

City 17 // Combine Occupation

OUR BENEFACTORS

Twenty years after the Black Mesa Incident, the Combine — a vast interdimensional empire — conquered Earth in the Seven Hour War. Humanity was subjugated, relocated to urban centers like City 17, and placed under the puppet administration of former Black Mesa administrator Dr. Wallace Breen.

The Resistance, led by figures like Eli Vance, Alyx Vance, and Barney Calhoun, fought from the shadows — until Gordon Freeman returned. Awakened by the enigmatic G-Man, Freeman became the symbol of the uprising: the Free Man, the One Free Man.

λ Timeline

FROM BLACK MESA TO CITY 17

200- // HALF-LIFE
The Black Mesa Incident

Dr. Gordon Freeman triggers the Resonance Cascade during an experiment with Sample GG-3883. Alien creatures from Xen invade. Freeman fights through the facility, defeats the Nihilanth on Xen, and is placed in stasis by the G-Man.

200- // SEVEN HOUR WAR
The Combine Invasion

The portal storms caused by the Resonance Cascade attract the Combine. Earth falls in just seven hours. Dr. Breen negotiates humanity's surrender and becomes Earth Administrator. The Combine begins suppressing human reproduction and draining Earth's resources.

~5 YEARS LATER // HALF-LIFE: ALYX
Alyx Vance & The Vault

A young Alyx Vance battles through the Quarantine Zone to rescue her father Eli from Combine forces. She discovers a Combine superweapon — the Vault — suspended above the city, only to find the G-Man imprisoned within. She frees him and strikes a fateful bargain.

~20 YEARS LATER // HALF-LIFE 2
Rise of the Resistance

The G-Man delivers Freeman to City 17. Reuniting with allies — Alyx, Barney, Eli, Kleiner — Freeman sparks the City 17 uprising. Armed with the gravity gun, he storms the Citadel, confronts Breen, and destroys the dark energy reactor. The Resistance fights on.

AFTERMATH // EPISODES
The Continuing Struggle

Freeman and Alyx escape the collapsing Citadel, battle through the outlands, and discover the Borealis — an Aperture Science research vessel lost in teleportation. The Combine launches a devastating counterattack. Eli Vance is killed by a Combine Advisor. The fight for Earth continues...
