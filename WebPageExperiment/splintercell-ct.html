OPSAT BriefingOPSAT 简报

作战卫星通讯Operational Satellite Uplink
●●● Mission Brief — Operation Chaos Theory ●●● 任务简报 — 混沌理论行动
Agent: Sam Fisher | Callsign: Panther
Handler: Irving Lambert | Tech: Anna Grímsdóttir

In 2007, Information Warfare has become the new battlefield. A series of algorithmic attacks threatens to destabilize global infrastructure. The Masse Kernels — weaponized algorithms — are being deployed against the United States and her allies. Japan, North Korea, and a shadowy PMC called Displace International are all connected.

Third Echelon deploys its most lethal operative, Sam Fisher, with full Fifth Freedom authorization. Fisher is sanctioned to spy, steal, destroy, and assassinate to protect American interests. From the Lighthouse of Talara to the Kokubo Sosho war room — every shadow is his weapon.

●●● CLASSIFICATION: ULTRA BLACK — EYES ONLY 特工：山姆·费舍尔 | 代号：黑豹
指挥官：欧文·兰伯特 | 技术支援：安娜·格里姆斯多蒂尔

2007年，信息战成为新战场。一系列算法攻击威胁着全球基础设施的稳定。马塞内核——武器化算法——正被部署来对付美国及其盟友。日本、朝鲜以及一个名为Displace International的神秘私人军事公司都牵涉其中。

第三梯队派出其最致命的特工山姆·费舍尔，授予完全的第五自由权限。费舍尔被授权从事间谍活动、窃取情报、摧毁目标和执行暗杀以保护美国利益。从塔拉拉灯塔到国防总省作战室——每一寸阴影都是他的武器。

●●● 密级：超黑 — 仅限阅览
●●● Sam Fisher — Operative Profile ●●● 山姆·费舍尔 — 特工档案
Name: Samuel Leo Fisher
DOB: August 17, 1957 | Status: ACTIVE — FIELD
Specialty: Infiltration, CQC, Electronic Warfare, SIGINT
Service Record: CIA (SAD/SOG), Navy SEALs (DEVGRU), Third Echelon (Splinter Cell)

Fisher is the prototype Splinter Cell — a lone operative who works in complete darkness, deniable by his own government. Chaos Theory represents his most complex operation: navigating a three-way geopolitical crisis between the US, Japan, and the Koreas while dismantling the Masse algorithm threat.

●●● Multi-Vision Goggles: Night Vision / Thermal / EEV capable 姓名：塞缪尔·利奥·费舍尔
出生日期：1957年8月17日 | 状态：现役 — 外勤
专长：潜入、近身格斗、电子战、信号情报
服役记录：中央情报局（特别活动中心）、海豹突击队（DEVGRU）、第三梯队（细胞分裂）

费舍尔是细胞分裂计划的原型特工——一名在完全黑暗中行动的独行特工，其政府可以否认其存在。混沌理论代表了他最复杂的行动：在美日韩三方地缘政治危机中穿行，同时瓦解马塞算法威胁。

Stealth Meters潜行仪表

光线与声音监测Light & Sound Monitoring
●●● LIGHT METER光线仪表
Ambient Light Detection环境光检测
SHADOW阴影 ●●● EXPOSED暴露
The light meter is Sam Fisher's most critical tool. Stay in darkness — keep the bar low. When the meter fills, guards can see you. Shoot out lights, stick to shadows, move through the dark. 光线仪表是山姆·费舍尔最关键的工具。保持在黑暗中——让指示条保持低位。当仪表填满时，守卫就能看到你。打碎灯光，贴紧阴影，在黑暗中移动。
●●● SOUND METER声音仪表
Acoustic Waveform Analysis声学波形分析
SILENT安静 ●●● LOUD噪音
Ambient noise masks your movement. Walk slowly on hard surfaces. Use environmental sound — rain, machinery, music — to cover footsteps. The waveform shows real-time audio detection levels. 环境噪音可以掩盖你的行动。在硬质地面上缓慢行走。利用环境声音——雨声、机器声、音乐——来掩盖脚步声。波形显示实时音频检测水平。

Sam Fisher faces his most critical decision — one that determines the fate of millions. The culmination of the greatest stealth game ever made. 最终任务。潜入日本国防总部以阻止第三次世界大战。I-SDF的作战室是阻止导弹发射的关键。山姆·费舍尔面临他最关键的抉择——一个决定数百万人命运的选择。有史以来最伟大潜行游戏的高潮。

Co-Op Operations合作行动

双人协同作战Two-Agent Cooperative Missions
●●● Cooperative Multiplayer — Classified ●●● 合作多人模式 — 机密
Chaos Theory introduced a revolutionary cooperative campaign. Two agents working in perfect synchronization — boosting each other over walls, performing dual interrogations, coordinating distractions. A completely separate storyline with unique missions that complement the single-player narrative. This was cooperative stealth done right, years before anyone else even attempted it. 混沌理论引入了革命性的合作战役。两名特工完美协作——互相翻越墙壁、双人审讯、协调转移注意力。一条完全独立的故事线，配有独特的任务来补充单人叙事。这是在其他人尝试之前多年就做到的正确的合作潜行。
●●● Dual Maneuvers●●● 双人战术
Tomoe Nage throw to reach high platforms. Human ladder for vertical access. Dual grab for synchronized takedowns. Co-op rappelling down building faces. Every move requires communication and trust. 巴投翻越技术登上高处平台。人梯获得垂直通道。双人同步制服。合作垂降建筑外墙。每一个动作都需要沟通和信任。
●●● Versus Mode●●● 对战模式
Spies vs. Mercenaries returns and improves on Pandora Tomorrow's foundation. Third-person spies with acrobatics and gadgets against first-person mercenaries with firepower and technology. The most intense asymmetric multiplayer ever created. 间谍对雇佣兵模式回归，在潘多拉明天的基础上改进。拥有杂技和小工具的第三人称间谍对抗拥有火力和科技的第一人称雇佣兵。有史以来最紧张的不对称多人游戏。
