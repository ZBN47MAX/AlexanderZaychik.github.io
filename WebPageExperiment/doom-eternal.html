HELL ON EARTH

Earth has been overrun. 60% of the human population has been consumed by the demonic invasion. The ARC, humanity's combined military force, has been shattered. Cities burn, the sky bleeds red, and the armies of Hell march unchallenged — until the Slayer arrives.

The Khan Maykr, divine ruler of Urdak, has made a pact with the Dark Lords of Hell. The Maykrs harvest human souls to fuel their own immortality through Argent Energy. The Sentinels — once protectors of the realms — are broken, their order corrupted from within.
