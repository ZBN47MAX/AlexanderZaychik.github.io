System Analysis 系统分析

The War Economy 战争经济

In 2014, the world's economy revolves entirely around war. Private Military Companies have become the world's largest industry, fueled by nanomachine-controlled soldiers and an arms market regulated by the Patriots' AI system. 2014年，世界经济完全围绕战争运转。私人军事公司已成为世界最大产业，由纳米机器控制的士兵和爱国者AI系统监管的军火市场推动。

Mission Briefing 任务简报

Five Acts 五幕

Old Snake's final mission spans five acts across the globe — from the Middle East to Shadow Moses — culminating in the destruction of the Patriot AI network. 老蛇的最终任务横跨全球五幕——从中东到影子摩西——最终摧毁爱国者AI网络。

Act 1
Liquid Sun
Middle East 中东
Snake infiltrates a war zone to assassinate Liquid Ocelot, encountering the horrors of the war economy firsthand. 蛇潜入战区刺杀液体山猫，亲眼目睹战争经济的恐怖。
Act 2
Solid Sun
South America 南美洲
Tracking Liquid through the jungle, Snake meets Drebin and witnesses the power of Liquid's insurrection. 在丛林中追踪液体，蛇遇到了德雷宾，目睹了液体叛乱的力量。
Act 3
Third Sun
Eastern Europe 东欧
A resistance operation in a European city. Snake tails a member to locate Big Mama and EVA. 欧洲城市中的抵抗行动。蛇跟踪成员找到大妈妈和EVA。
Act 4
Twin Suns
Shadow Moses 影子摩西
A haunting return to Shadow Moses Island. Memories of 2005, REX vs. RAY, and the ghost of a legend. 重返影子摩西岛的令人难忘之旅。2005年的记忆，REX对RAY，以及传说的幽灵。
Act 5
Old Sun
Outer Haven 外天堂
The final assault on Liquid's fortress. The microwave corridor. The fall of the Patriots. The end. 对液体堡垒的最终攻击。微波走廊。爱国者的陨落。终结。
Enemy Profile 敌人档案

Beauty & Beast Unit 美女与野兽部队

Four women driven mad by the horrors of war, encased in mechanical beast armor. Each embodies an emotion of the battlefield — each echoes a legend from Snake's past. 四位被战争恐怖逼疯的女性，被机械野兽装甲包裹。每人都体现了战场上的一种情感——每人都呼应了蛇过去的一个传奇。

I
Laughing 狂笑
Octopus 章鱼
ECHO: DECOY OCTOPUS 回响：诱饵章鱼
Master of disguise with OctoCamo-like tentacles. Her laughter masks the trauma of watching her family slaughtered — a mind broken by unimaginable cruelty, hiding behind mimicry. 拥有类似OctoCamo触手的伪装大师。她的笑声掩盖了目睹家人被屠杀的创伤——一个被难以想象的残忍所摧毁的心灵，藏身于模仿之后。
II
Raging 狂怒
Raven 乌鸦
ECHO: VULCAN RAVEN 回响：火神乌鸦
Airborne fury in winged armor raining grenades from above. Her rage was born when she was forced to become a child soldier — anger is all she has left. 穿着翼甲从空中倾泻手榴弹的空中狂怒。她的愤怒源于被迫成为童兵——愤怒是她仅存的一切。
III
Crying 哭泣
Wolf 狼
ECHO: SNIPER WOLF 回响：狙击狼
A railgun-wielding beast prowling the Alaskan snowfields. Her tears never stop — she wept as she was forced to kill refugees alongside the very wolves that raised her. 一只在阿拉斯加雪原上游荡的持轨道枪野兽。她的泪水永不停止——当她被迫与养育她的狼群一起杀害难民时，她哭泣不止。
IV
Screaming 尖叫
Mantis 螳螂
ECHO: PSYCHO MANTIS 回响：精神螳螂
Puppeteer who controls corpses through nanomachines. Her screams are unending — the psychic residue of Psycho Mantis himself compels her, a vessel for a dead man's will. 通过纳米机器控制尸体的傀儡师。她的尖叫永无止境——精神螳螂本人的灵能残留驱使着她，一个死者意志的容器。
Network Architecture 网络架构

SOP System SOP系统

Sons of the Patriots — the nanomachine network that links every soldier on the battlefield. It controls emotions, regulates weapons, and feeds data to the Patriot AIs. When it falls, the world goes mad. 爱国者之子——连接战场上每个士兵的纳米机器网络。它控制情绪、管理武器、向爱国者AI传输数据。当它崩溃时，世界陷入疯狂。

Nanomachine Integration 纳米机器整合

Every PMC soldier is injected with nanomachines that regulate their hormones, suppress fear, enhance reflexes, and report battlefield data in real-time. The system creates perfect soldiers — but at the cost of their humanity. 每个PMC士兵都被注入纳米机器，调节激素、抑制恐惧、增强反射、实时报告战场数据。系统创造了完美的士兵——但代价是他们的人性。

The Patriot AIs 爱国者AI

Five AIs — GW, TJ, AL, TR, and the core JD — form the Patriots' proxy system. Built from the framework of The Boss's will, they were meant to unite the world. Instead, they became its invisible tyrants. FOXALIVE destroys them all. 五个AI——GW、TJ、AL、TR和核心JD——构成爱国者的代理系统。基于The Boss的意志框架构建，本意是团结世界。然而，它们变成了隐形暴君。FOXALIVE将其全部摧毁。
