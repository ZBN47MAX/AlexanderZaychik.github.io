The IBM Personal Computer, model 5150, was introduced on August 12, 1981. It featured an Intel 8088 processor running at 4.77 MHz, 16 KB of RAM (expandable to 256 KB on the motherboard), and one or two 5.25-inch floppy disk drives.

IBM 个人计算机 5150 型于 1981 年 8 月 12 日推出。它配备了 运行在 4.77 MHz 的 Intel 8088 处理器、16 KB RAM（可在主板 上扩展到 256 KB）以及一个或两个 5.25 英寸软盘驱动器。

The machine ran PC-DOS (later MS-DOS), a command-line operating system that would dominate the personal computer landscape for over a decade. Its open architecture allowed third-party expansion, giving rise to the "IBM Compatible" ecosystem.

该机器运行 PC-DOS（后来的 MS-DOS），一个命令行操作系统， 它在接下来的十多年中主导了个人计算机领域。其开放式架构允许 第三方扩展，催生了"IBM 兼容机"生态系统。

Common DOS commands included DIR, COPY, DEL, TYPE, FORMAT, CHKDSK, and EDLIN (the line editor). Power users memorized CONFIG.SYS and AUTOEXEC.BAT optimizations to squeeze every byte from the 640 KB conventional memory limit.

常用的 DOS 命令包括 DIR、COPY、DEL、TYPE、FORMAT、CHKDSK 和 EDLIN（行编辑器）。高级用户会记住 CONFIG.SYS 和 AUTOEXEC.BAT 的优化方法，以从 640 KB 常规内存限制中 挤出每一个字节。

"640K ought to be enough for anybody." "640K 对任何人来说都应该够用了。"
─ Attributed to Bill Gates (likely apocryphal) ─ 据传比尔·盖茨所说（可能为杜撰）

Vintage hardware such as the Model F keyboard, MDA and CGA display adapters, and the iconic beige case design defined an era. The green (P1) and amber (P3) phosphor monitors gave terminals their characteristic glow ─ recreated on this very page.

Model F 键盘、MDA 和 CGA 显示适配器以及标志性的米色机箱 设计等复古硬件定义了一个时代。绿色 (P1) 和琥珀色 (P3) 荧光 显示器赋予了终端独特的光晕效果 ─ 正如本页面所重现的那样。
