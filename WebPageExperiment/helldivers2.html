TERMINIDS
THREAT LEVEL ★★★★☆

Insectoid organisms of terrifying scale and ferocity. From the skittering Scavengers to the towering Bile Titans, these creatures exist only to consume and multiply. Originally farmed for Element 710, the Terminid outbreak now threatens entire sectors.

⚔ TACTICAL ADVISORY
Prioritize fire-based stratagems. Aim for soft underbelly on armored variants. Bug Breaches indicate hive proximity — prepare for swarm tactics.
🤖
AUTOMATONS
THREAT LEVEL ★★★★★

Mechanical horrors driven by a fanatical hatred of organic life. The Automaton Collective fields devastating war machines from nimble Scout Striders to the dreaded Factory Strider. They fight with cold precision and will not stop until every human is exterminated.

⚔ TACTICAL ADVISORY
Anti-tank weapons essential. Target head units for quick kills. Beware of Hulk variants — flank and strike rear vents. Dropships are priority targets to prevent reinforcement.
👁
ILLUMINATES
THREAT LEVEL ★★★★★

An ancient and technologically superior alien species that wields psionic energy and advanced energy weapons. The Illuminates consider themselves the rightful rulers of the galaxy and view humanity as an obstacle to their grand design. Their return signals a new era of galactic conflict.

⚔ TACTICAL ADVISORY
Energy shields require sustained fire or EMP-class weapons. Beware psionic illusions — trust your HUD, not your eyes. Obelisks must be destroyed to prevent area denial. Coordinate squad fire on Council Members.
