You are a detective with a spectacular hangover. The world is your case, your mind is the weapon, and every thought is a suspect. Solve the murder. Solve yourself.

The City

Revachol

Once the capital of the world, now a wounded beast licking its scars. Revachol is a city of faded grandeur and stubborn decay — where revolutionary dreams rot in the rain and neon signs flicker over empty ideals.

SHIVERS [Medium: Success]
The wind carries voices from Martinaise. Salt, diesel, old regret. The harbour groans under the weight of fifty years of foreign occupation. The city remembers — even when you cannot.

Martinaise is the district where it all happens: a fishing village turned slum, occupied by the Coalition, haunted by the spectre of the Commune. Your case begins and ends here.

Investigation

The Hanged Man

A body hangs from a tree behind the Whirling-in-Rags hostel. The murder is days old. The evidence is rotting. And you — you can't even remember your own name.

Victim
The Hanged Man

A mercenary from an unknown private military company. Shot, then strung up in a tree. The killing was personal — but whose person?

Location
The Whirling-in-Rags

A hostel-turned-headquarters. The cafeteria manager Garte would like you to pay your bill. The body in the backyard is bad for business.

Partner
Lieutenant Kim Kitsuragi

Your partner from Precinct 57. Methodical, patient, quietly compassionate. The best cop in Revachol — and your only anchor to reality.

Context
The Strike

The dockworkers' union holds Martinaise in a stranglehold. The murder intersects with labour politics, corporate greed, and decades of festering resentment.

Four Ways to Fail the World

Communism
Mazovian Socio-Economics

The revolution failed. The Commune burned. But the dream persists, stubborn as a wound that won't close. You can build it again — in your heart, if nowhere else.

"0.000% of communism has been built."

Ultraliberalism
Indirect Modes of Taxation

Money is freedom. The market is God. You can hustle, grind, and monetize your way to meaning — or at least to a nice pair of shoes.

"I want to have fuck-you money."

Moralism
Kingdom of Conscience

Centrism as ideology. The Coalition keeps the world stable through measured intervention and quiet control. It's not exciting. It's not inspiring. But the alternative is chaos.

"Moralism is the most harmful ideology."
☠
Fascism
Revacholian Nationhood

Nostalgia weaponized. Fascism in Revachol is a pathetic, self-pitying nationalism — a longing for lost glory that never quite existed. The game does not let you forget what it really means.

"Measurehead approved."
