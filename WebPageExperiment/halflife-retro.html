Safety Protocols 安全规程

◆
⚠ MANDATORY SAFETY GUIDELINES ⚠

1. All Level 3+ personnel must wear HEV suits in test chambers at all times.
2. Report any unusual resonance cascade readings to your sector supervisor immediately.
3. Headcrabs are NOT pets. Do not attempt to domesticate fauna from Xen.
4. Emergency evacuation routes are posted in every corridor. Know your nearest exit.
5. The on-site nuclear warhead is for EMERGENCY USE ONLY (see Administrator Breen).
6. Do not look directly into the Anti-Mass Spectrometer beam.
7. Security doors must remain locked during Code Red. No exceptions.
8. All inter-dimensional samples must be stored in quarantine lockers. ⚠ 强制性安全准则 ⚠

1. 所有三级及以上人员在测试室内必须始终穿戴HEV防护服。
2. 如发现异常谐振级联读数，立即报告所在区域主管。
3. 猎头蟹不是宠物。禁止驯化来自Xen的生物。
4. 每条走廊均张贴紧急疏散路线图，请熟知最近出口。
5. 现场核弹头仅供紧急情况使用（详情咨询布林行政长官）。
6. 请勿直视反质量光谱仪光束。
7. 红色警报期间安全门必须保持锁定，无一例外。
8. 所有跨维度样本须存放于隔离储物柜中。
