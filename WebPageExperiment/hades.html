The Underworld

A realm of darkness, death, and defiance

👑

The House of Hades

The central hub of the Underworld where Hades himself presides. Here, Zagreus plots his escape, speaks with shades and allies, and prepares for each attempt to reach the surface.

💀

Death is Not the End

Each death returns Zagreus to the House of Hades, but with new knowledge, relationships, and power. The story unfolds across countless escape attempts, revealing deeper truths with every run.

⚡

Olympian Aid

The gods of Olympus reach out to Zagreus, offering their blessings to aid his escape. Each god grants unique powers that can be combined in countless ways to create devastating builds.

Infernal Arms

Six legendary weapons forged for the war against the Titans

⚔️

Stygius

Blade of the Underworld

A versatile sword with fast, sweeping strikes. Perfect for aggressive close-range combat and rapid combo chains.

🔱

Varatha

Eternal Spear

A long-reaching spear that can be thrown and recalled. Excellent for maintaining distance while punishing foes.

🛡️

Aegis

Shield of Chaos

A shield that blocks all frontal attacks and can be thrown like a discus, bouncing between enemies.

🏹

Coronacht

Heart-Seeking Bow

A powerful bow with charged shots and a special volley attack. Masters of range keep enemies at bay.

💀

Malphon

Twin Fists

Blazing-fast twin fists for relentless close-quarters combat. Unleash devastating rapid-fire punches.

🔫

Exagryph

Adamant Rail

A rapid-fire ranged weapon with a special grenade launcher. The most unconventional of the Infernal Arms.

Realms of the Dead

Four treacherous regions stand between Zagreus and freedom

🟢

Tartarus

The deepest pit of the Underworld. Green-tinted halls of stone and bone, home to the Wretched and lesser shades. The Fury Sisters — Megaera, Alecto, and Tisiphone — guard the exit.

🔴

Asphodel

A scorching landscape of lava rivers and floating islands. Once beautiful meadows, now a burning wasteland. The Bone Hydra — Lernie — awaits as the region's fearsome guardian.

🟡

Elysium

The paradise reserved for legendary heroes. Golden halls and grand arenas where the greatest warriors of myth eternally spar. Theseus and the Minotaur defend this blessed realm.

🌿

Temple of Styx

The final gauntlet before the surface. A labyrinthine temple infested with vermin, standing at the border between the Underworld and the mortal realm. Cerberus guards the final door.
