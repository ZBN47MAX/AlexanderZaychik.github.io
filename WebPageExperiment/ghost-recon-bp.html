Archipelago Overview

Auroa is a fictional South Pacific archipelago purchased by Skell Technology CEO Jace Skell. Once a tech utopia designed to advance humanitarian drone technology, the island has been seized by the Wolves — a rogue faction of former Ghosts led by Lt. Col. Cole D. Walker. All communications have been severed. Civilian population trapped. The island's diverse terrain spans volcanic peaks, dense rainforests, frozen fjords, and arid salt flats.

▲ Skell Technology HQ

The glass and steel nerve center of Jace Skell's drone empire. Now a Wolves command post controlling the island's autonomous defense network.

▲ Erewhon Cave

Hidden deep in the mountains, this natural cave system serves as the Homesteaders' last refuge — a base for the resistance and your only safe haven on Auroa.

▲ Restricted Zone

Sector 3 — designated no-go area. Highest drone concentration on the island. Azrael surveillance drones on permanent patrol. Only the most skilled Ghosts venture here.

ENEMY FACTION

The Wolves

Former Ghost operatives turned rogue. The Wolves are a paramilitary force that has seized control of Auroa, wielding Skell Technology's drone arsenal against their former brothers-in-arms.

COMMANDING OFFICER

Cole D. Walker

Lt. Colonel. Former Ghost team leader and brother-in-arms to Nomad. Walker became disillusioned with the government's use of soldiers as expendable assets. He seized Auroa to build an army beyond any nation's control — the Wolves. Brilliant tactician, ruthless leader, and the most dangerous enemy the Ghosts have ever faced.

WOLF LIEUTENANT

Josiah Hill

Walker's most loyal enforcer. Former Delta Force operator who followed Walker without question. Oversees the Wolves' ground operations and maintains brutal discipline within the ranks. Controls Auroa's eastern provinces with an iron fist.

WOLF LIEUTENANT

Rosebud

The Wolves' intelligence officer. Expert in electronic warfare and surveillance systems. She controls Auroa's drone network and communications blackout. Former NSA analyst who was recruited by Walker for her ability to weaponize information and technology against any target.

THREAT: MEDIUM

Azrael

High-altitude surveillance drone. Patrols set routes across Auroa, scanning for heat signatures. If detected, it calls in Wolves reinforcements immediately. Go prone, use terrain cover, or time your movements between sweeps.

THREAT: HIGH

Aamon

Ground-based tank drone. Heavily armored quadrupedal platform with mounted chain gun. Patrols key installations. Requires EMP grenades or sustained fire on weak points. Fighting one head-on without preparation is suicide.

THREAT: MEDIUM

Murmur

Small rotary attack drone deployed in swarms. Individually weak but overwhelming in numbers. They strafe with light weapons fire and can flush targets from cover. Area-denial weapons and shotguns are most effective against swarms.

THREAT: EXTREME

Behemoth

The apex predator of Skell's arsenal. Massive autonomous tank with missile launchers, anti-personnel guns, and near-impenetrable armor. Requires coordinated team tactics, precise weak-point targeting, and heavy ordnance. Each Behemoth guards a critical sector of Auroa.

FIELD MANUAL

Survival Systems

☤

Injury System

Wounds are persistent and debilitating. Gunshots, falls, and explosions cause specific injuries — limping, reduced aim stability, stamina drain. Field bandages provide temporary relief; only a bivouac or syringe kit can fully heal. Manage your injuries or die trying.

☢

Bivouac

Scattered across Auroa, bivouacs are hidden campsites where Ghosts can rest, heal, craft items, change class, and prepare for the next operation. The campfire provides warmth and recovery — a brief respite from the cold brutality of the island. Choose your preparations wisely before heading out.

⚙

Crafting

Gather resources from the environment — plants for rations, metal for weapon parts, chemicals for explosives. Craft bandages, rations, water bottles, and sync shot drones in the field. Resource management is the difference between a successful mission and a body left in the jungle.
