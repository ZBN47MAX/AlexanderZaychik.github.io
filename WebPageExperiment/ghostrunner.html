DHARMA TOWER 达摩塔

Humanity's Last Refuge 人类最后的避难所

Dharma Tower stands as the final bastion of human civilization after a global cataclysm rendered the outside world uninhabitable. Ruled by the tyrannical Keymaster — known as Mara — the tower's inhabitants are stratified into rigid social classes, with the wealthy elite occupying the summit while the impoverished masses toil in the lower depths.

达摩塔是全球大灾难使外部世界无法居住后，人类文明最后的堡垒。在暴虐的密钥大师——玛拉的统治下，塔的居民被严格划分为社会阶层，富裕的精英占据塔顶，而贫困的大众在底层辛苦劳作。

The Architect, the tower's creator, was betrayed and killed by Mara. Now his consciousness persists in the cybervoid, guiding Jack — the last Ghostrunner — on a mission to ascend the tower and overthrow the tyrant.

建筑师，塔的创造者，被玛拉背叛杀害。现在他的意识存留在赛博虚空中，引导杰克——最后的幽灵行者——执行攀登塔顶推翻暴君的任务。
